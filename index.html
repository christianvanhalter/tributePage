Andy Warhol

The Andy Warhol Museum, Pittsburgh, United States

    Here's a time line of Andy Warhol's life:

  • [1928] BORN
    - Born in Pittsburgh, United States. Son of Russian immigrants. Andrei Warhola, coal miner and Julia Zavacky.
  • [1937] 9 YEARS
    - He contracts an illness and for long periods in bed with his mother, who teaches him how to draw.
  • [1945] 17 YEARS
    - Study advertising illustration at the Carnegie Mellon Institute in Pittsburgh.
  • [1949] 21 YEARS
    - He makes store cabinets in Pittsburgh and travels to New York for the first time.
  • [1950] 22 YEARS
    - His illustrations appear in magazines and showcases throughout New York.
  • [1952] 24 YEARS
    - First individual exhibition.
    - His mother travels to New York to live with him.
  • [1953] 25 YEARS
    - He begins to lose his hair and begins to wear wigs.
  • [1954 - 1956] 26 - 28 YEARS
    - Publishes illustrated books.
    - The shoe company I. Miller hires him to illustrate his notices.
  • [1957 - 1958] 29 - 30 YEARS
    - Andy Warhol Enterprises sleeve to manage the orders of its customers.
    - He is one of the most sought-after illustrators of the moment.
  • [1960 - 1962] 32 - 34 YEARS
    - First works of Pop Art: Coca-Cola bottles, Campbell soup and dollars.
    - Start the series Death and disaster.
    - He works on his series Marilyn.
  • [1966] 38 YEARS
    - Design the cover of the Velvet Underground album with a banana with a self-adhesive shell.
  • [1969] 41 YEARS
    - Andy Warhol, a trademark, is associated with Air France, Braniff Airlines and Pioneer.
    - He founded the Interview magazine that gives an account of New York's social life and circulates to the present.
  • [1974] 46 YEARS
    - Create the Time Capsules where the artist collects the most diverse objects, from a piece of cake and even a mummified foot from Ancient Egypt.
  • [1975 - 1976] 47 - 48 YEARS
    - He frequents the legendary Studio 54.
    - The philosophy of Andy Warhol is published (From A to B and Back Again).
    - He makes portraits on request.
  • [1980] 52 YEARS
    - Meeting with Pope John Paul II.
    - Produces the television show Andy Warhol's TV.
    - He makes the series Ten portraits of Jews of the twentieth century.
  • [1987] 22 - 59 YEARS
    - Andy Warhol dies at the New York Hospital after having undergone a routine gallbladder operation.
    - More than two thousand people attend his funeral at St Patrick's Cathedral in New York.
